Heritage Sites

According to the United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization (UNESCO) a heritage site is an location that is a historical site, a building, or an area of the unspoilt natural environment, considered to be important to a country or area's heritage.
Sri Lanka has a few heritage sites such as the Ancient City of Polonnaruwa, the Ancient City of Sigiriya, the Ancient City of Kandy, the Ancient City of Anuradhapura, Sinharaja Forest Reserve, Rangiri Dambulla Cave Temple, Central Highlands of Sri Lanka, and Old Town of Galle and its Fortifications.
