Activities in Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka offers foreign visitors from all over the world a myriad of activities to take part in and make their stay fun, memorable and adventourous as much as possible.
These activities can range from hiking, wildlife safari, rock climbing to more exciting activities such as hot air balloon rides over Sri Lanka, or going on tours to some of Sri Lanka's heritage and religious sites to indulge in the Sri Lankan cultural aspect.
Sri Lanka being an island in the Indian Ocean there is no shortage of water-based activities for visitors to engage in either. These activities include but are not limited to jet skiing, snorkelling, diving, whale and dolphin watching, surfing, and white water rafting.

Hiking

Any direction a person turns to in Sri Lanka will be treated with the view of a rugged, rocky mountain, so it would come as no surprise that Sri Lanka has many hiking destinations on offer for adventure seekers. From peaceful, walk-in-the-park type routes to bumpy pathways, there are varying kinds of hiking trails on offer around the country.

  1. Location(s): Adams's Peak, Knuckles Range, Pidurangala, Ella Rock, Horton Plains and World's End
  2. Availability: best time to visit would be early hours of the day, usually between 7am-3pm
  3. Duration: 3 hours to half a day
  4. Cost: free

Hot air balloon

What better way to be captivated by the beautiful scenery of Sri Lanka than to get on a hot air balloon and soar over the various mountains, lakes, rivers, and trees like a bird, and in one place in Sri Lanka it is possible to commandeer a hot air balloon and do exactly this.

  1. Location(s): Dambulla - Kandalama
  2. Availability: early hours of the day
  3. Duration: 1 hour and 20 mins
  4. Cost: Rs. 56,000-522,000 per person

Explore Sri Lankan cuisine

One way to fully immerse oneself in the Sri Lankan atmosphere than to feast on some of the authentic Sri Lankan dishes. From the iconic 'kottu roti' to milky, mouth watering 'kiribath' served with 'sambol' there are many dishes to satisfy one's taste buds.

  1. Location(s): restaurants all over the country
  2. Availability: anytime of the day
  3. Duration: -
  4. Cost: varies from place to place

Wildlife Safari

Sri Lanka is home to majestic wild animals such as elephant, leopards, peacocks, and a host of many other animals, and a wildlife safari in Sri Lanka provides an authentic wildlife experience.
Sri Lanka is rated as one of the ten global bio diverse hotspots in the world and as Asia's foremost wildlife experience.

  1. Location(s): Sinharaja, Yala National Park, Wilpattu National Park, Horton Plains, Kitulgala Forest Reserve
  2. Availability: early hours of the day
  3. Duration: whole day
  4. Cost: Rs. 130,000-245,000 per person

Cultural tours in Sri Lanka

A cultural tour in a country can provide a person a more in-depth view into the the cultural landscape of that place.
Sri Lanka being a land of cultural diversity there is a great variety of cultural sites to visit. In Sri Lanka these tours can inlcude a visit to a natural site such as Sigiriya or a heritage site such as the Anuradhapura city.

  1. Location(s): Anuradhapura, Kandy, Galle, Colombo
  2. Availability: all year round
  3. Duration: whole day
  4. Cost: Rs. 100,000-350,000 per person
